Draft a planning brief for: $@

Fill in the following sections concretely, then hand the result to `/se-plan`. Keep each section honest — write "unknown, decide during planning" instead of inventing.

## Problem statement

What pain or opportunity does this address? Who experiences it? Why now?

## Desired outcome

What does the world look like if this lands well? Name observable, falsifiable changes.

## Scope

In-scope and out-of-scope bullets. Anything you would not be surprised to defer goes in out-of-scope.

## Constraints

Hard rules the plan must respect (existing contracts, security, perf, deadline, team capacity).

## Open questions

Decisions that must be made during planning. Tag with `(blocker)` if the plan cannot reach a unit list without resolving them.

## Suggested entry point

If you have a hypothesis for the smallest valuable vertical slice, name it. The planner can confirm or revise.
